About Us

Building exceptional businesses through strategic investment, operational excellence, and unwavering commitment to long-term value creation.

Our Story

Zaitsev Holding Group, LLC was established with a clear vision: to identify, acquire, and develop businesses with exceptional potential across diverse industries. Based in Wyoming, we leverage the state's business-friendly environment while maintaining a national perspective on investment opportunities.

Our approach combines the discipline of private equity with the long-term perspective of a family office. We're not just investorsâ€”we're active partners committed to the success and growth of every business in our portfolio. This hands-on approach allows us to add value beyond capital, bringing operational expertise, strategic guidance, and industry connections to each venture.

What sets us apart is our commitment to sustainable growth. We believe that the best businesses are built on strong fundamentals, ethical practices, and a genuine commitment to serving customers and communities. This philosophy guides every decision we make and every partnership we forge.

Our Mission

To build and manage a portfolio of exceptional businesses that create lasting value for stakeholders, employees, and communities. We achieve this through strategic acquisitions, operational improvements, and a relentless focus on excellence in everything we do.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier holding company that transforms good businesses into great ones, setting new standards for operational excellence, ethical business practices, and sustainable growth across all industries we serve.

Why Wyoming?

Wyoming offers a unique combination of advantages for holding companies and private equity firms. The state's business-friendly regulatory environment, favorable tax structure, and strong legal protections make it an ideal location for our headquarters.

Beyond the practical benefits, Wyoming embodies values that align with our own: independence, integrity, and a pioneering spirit. The state's commitment to supporting business growth while maintaining high ethical standards reflects our approach to building and managing companies.

From this strategic base, we maintain the flexibility to pursue opportunities nationwide while benefiting from Wyoming's supportive business ecosystem and quality of life.

Our Core Values

These principles guide our decisions, shape our culture, and define our approach to business.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency and honesty in all our dealings. Our reputation is built on trust, and we never compromise our ethical standards for short-term gains.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every aspect of our operations. From strategic planning to daily execution, we set high standards and continuously work to exceed them.

Innovation

We embrace change and actively seek innovative solutions to challenges. Our willingness to adapt and evolve keeps our portfolio companies competitive and relevant.

Partnership

We believe in the power of collaboration. We work closely with management teams, employees, and stakeholders to achieve shared goals and mutual success.

People First

Our success depends on talented, motivated people. We invest in our teams, foster inclusive environments, and create opportunities for growth and development.

Sustainability

We take a long-term view in all our investments. We build businesses that can thrive for generations while being responsible stewards of resources and communities.

Our Investment Approach

A disciplined, strategic methodology that identifies opportunities and creates value.

Identification

We actively seek businesses with strong fundamentals, growth potential, and alignment with our strategic vision. Our rigorous screening process evaluates market position, financial health, management quality, and competitive advantages.

Due Diligence

We conduct comprehensive analysis of every potential acquisition, examining financial performance, operational efficiency, market dynamics, legal compliance, and growth opportunities. This thorough approach minimizes risk and maximizes potential.

Value Creation

Post-acquisition, we work closely with management teams to implement strategic improvements, optimize operations, expand market reach, and drive sustainable growth. Our hands-on approach ensures continuous value creation.

Zaitsev Holding Group, LLC is a Wyoming-based private equity and holding company committed to building exceptional businesses and creating lasting value.
