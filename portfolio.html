HLPFL Co

HLPFL Co transforms music career chaos into clarity by helping independent musicians build legally solid, privacy-first businesses with standout brands. From entity filing to contracts, web design to strategic guidance, HLPFL provides complete music business solutions that turn musical talent into thriving, legally compliant businesses.

Core Services

  • Artist Branding: Visual identity, professional websites, and marketing materials
  • Music Rights & Royalties: Copyright protection and royalty collection setup
  • Career Growth: Business strategy and market positioning frameworks
  • Tour & Event Management: Performance logistics and revenue optimization
  • Team Building: Connecting artists with managers, agents, and support staff

Value Proposition

HLPFL's comprehensive approach ensures independent musicians have everything they need to protect their art and maximize their income. The company's tagline "Create, Record, Profit, Repeat" embodies its mission to help artists turn their dreams into reality through legally sound business structures and strategic career planning.

Market Position

As a music industry veteran-led company, HLPFL leverages hard-earned experience to help musicians build resilient business structures. The company offers free resources including the "Music Industry Red Flags Every Independent Artist Must Know" guide, demonstrating its commitment to educating and empowering independent artists in an often complex industry.

Food & Beverage Technology

Takeout

Takeout is an innovative food and beverage delivery service that reimagines the delivery ecosystem by creating a more equitable platform for all stakeholders. Unlike traditional delivery services, Takeout pays drivers better wages than competitors while taking a smaller percentage from merchants, creating a sustainable model that benefits everyone involved.

The platform addresses fundamental inequities in the food delivery industry by prioritizing fair compensation for drivers and reasonable fees for restaurants. This balanced approach ensures quality service for customers while supporting the livelihoods of delivery drivers and the profitability of local restaurants.

Key Differentiators

  • Higher driver wages compared to industry competitors
  • Lower merchant fees to support restaurant profitability
  • Equitable platform model benefiting all stakeholders
  • Focus on sustainable business practices

Market Innovation

Takeout challenges the status quo in food delivery by proving that a more equitable model can be both sustainable and competitive. By reducing the financial burden on restaurants and improving driver compensation, the platform creates a healthier ecosystem that supports local food businesses and gig economy workers.

Vision

The company's vision extends beyond simple food delivery to creating a platform that demonstrates how technology can be used to build more equitable business models. Takeout proves that profitability and fairness are not mutually exclusive, setting a new standard for the delivery industry.

OnlyGainz began as an innovative platform combining elements of OnlyFans and Patreon, specifically designed for bodybuilding coaches to monetize their expertise within a niche community. The platform provided fitness professionals with tools to build subscription-based businesses, offering personalized coaching, training programs, and exclusive content to their dedicated followers.

Recognizing the broader potential of the subscription-based creator model, OnlyGainz evolved into RVNU, a Reddit-inspired community platform where everyone can be a creator. RVNU democratizes content monetization by allowing individuals to turn their hobbies and passions into full-time careers through subscription-based communities.

Platform Evolution

  • OnlyGainz: Niche platform for bodybuilding coaches and fitness professionals
  • RVNU: Expanded to all-encompassing creator economy platform
  • Subscription-based community model for sustainable creator income
  • Empowers individuals to monetize their expertise and passions

RVNU Vision

RVNU represents the next evolution of the creator economy, combining the community aspects of Reddit with the monetization capabilities of subscription platforms. The platform enables creators across all niches to build sustainable businesses around their passions, whether that's fitness, cooking, art, technology, or any other interest area.

Impact on Creator Economy

By providing tools for anyone to become a creator and monetize their expertise, RVNU addresses the growing demand for alternative income streams and passion-based careers. The platform's subscription model ensures creators can build predictable, sustainable income while fostering engaged communities around shared interests.

Mental Health & Apparel

Grippy Sox Club

Grippy Sox Club started as a clever play on the grippy socks given to patients at inpatient rehab facilities, transforming a symbol of mental health treatment into a powerful brand that reduces stigma around mental health. What began as a creative concept has evolved into a comprehensive direct entry package program for mental institutions nationwide.

The company provides mental health facilities with complete merch packages and high-quality clothing that patients actually want to wear. By creating appealing, branded merchandise associated with mental health treatment, Grippy Sox Club has helped normalize conversations around mental health and reduce the stigma that often prevents people from seeking help.

Program Offerings

  • Direct entry package programs for mental institutions
  • High-quality, appealing merchandise and clothing
  • Nationwide partnerships with mental health facilities
  • Stigma reduction through positive brand association

Social Impact

Grippy Sox Club's innovative approach to mental health merchandise has had a measurable impact on reducing stigma around mental health treatment. By creating products that patients are proud to wear and use, the company helps normalize mental health care and encourages more people to seek the help they need without shame or embarrassment.

Business Model

The direct entry package program provides mental institutions with turnkey merchandise solutions, making it easy for facilities to offer quality products to their patients. This B2B model has enabled rapid scaling across the country while maintaining the brand's core mission of destigmatizing mental health treatment through thoughtful, appealing design.
