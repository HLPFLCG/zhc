HLPFL: The Complete Artist Platform

HLPFL represents a revolutionary approach to the music industry with a 50/50 artist partnership model, 100% artist-first positioning, and mission-driven operations. Through HLPFL Records and the HLPFL technology platform, we're transforming how artists build sustainable careers while maintaining full ownership of their creative work.

The HLPFL ecosystem includes HLPFL Records (a mission-driven record label), HLPFL Co technology platform (unified tools for artists), BioBetter (link-in-bio platform), and a comprehensive artist portal with social media management, analytics, and AI-powered assistance. Built with Next.js 14, React, TypeScript, and enterprise-grade security.

Core Business Units

  • HLPFL Records: Record label with 50/50 artist partnerships, transparent royalty management, and comprehensive artist support
  • HLPFL Co Platform: Unified technology platform with social media management, analytics, form builder, and AI assistant
  • BioBetter: White-label link-in-bio platform with customizable social media tools and analytics
  • Artist Portal: Single sign-on access to all HLPFL tools, dashboards, and resources
  • Developer API: Usage-based API access for third-party integrations and custom solutions

Platform Features

  • Social Media Manager (hlpfl.space) - Post scheduling, calendar management, and multi-platform support
  • Link-in-Bio Manager (hlpfl.net) - Customizable landing pages with click tracking and analytics
  • Form Builder (hlpfl.com) - Custom forms with submission tracking and data collection
  • Analytics Dashboard - Unified metrics across all platforms with real-time insights
  • AI Chatbot Manager - White-label chatbot creation and management with advanced AI capabilities
  • Internal AI Assistant - LLM-powered support for artists and business operations

Technology Stack & Infrastructure

Built with modern technologies for maximum performance, scalability, and security. The platform achieves 99.9% uptime, sub-2-second load times, and 200ms API response times with 90+ mobile Lighthouse scores. Committed to 100% GDPR/CCPA compliance and PCI DSS Level 1 payment security.

Business Model

HLPFL operates with a unique 50/50 artist partnership model where artists retain full ownership of their masters and creative work. The mission-driven, artist-first approach prioritizes artist success above all else. Multiple revenue streams include label operations, technology platform subscriptions, API usage fees, and premium services.

Domain Portfolio

Food & Beverage Technology

Takeout

Takeout is an innovative food and beverage delivery service that reimagines the delivery ecosystem by creating a more equitable platform for all stakeholders. Unlike traditional delivery services, Takeout pays drivers better wages than competitors while taking a smaller percentage from merchants, creating a sustainable model that benefits everyone involved.

The platform addresses fundamental inequities in the food delivery industry by prioritizing fair compensation for drivers and reasonable fees for restaurants. This balanced approach ensures quality service for customers while supporting the livelihoods of delivery drivers and the profitability of local restaurants.

Key Differentiators

  • Higher driver wages compared to industry competitors
  • Lower merchant fees to support restaurant profitability
  • Equitable platform model benefiting all stakeholders
  • Focus on sustainable business practices

Market Innovation

Takeout challenges the status quo in food delivery by proving that a more equitable model can be both sustainable and competitive. By reducing the financial burden on restaurants and improving driver compensation, the platform creates a healthier ecosystem that supports local food businesses and gig economy workers.

Vision

The company's vision extends beyond simple food delivery to creating a platform that demonstrates how technology can be used to build more equitable business models. Takeout proves that profitability and fairness are not mutually exclusive, setting a new standard for the delivery industry.

Mental Health & Apparel

Grippy Sox Club

Grippy Sox Club started as a clever play on the grippy socks given to patients at inpatient rehab facilities, transforming a symbol of mental health treatment into a powerful brand that reduces stigma around mental health. What began as a creative concept has evolved into a comprehensive direct entry package program for mental institutions nationwide.

The company provides mental health facilities with complete merch packages and high-quality clothing that patients actually want to wear. By creating appealing, branded merchandise associated with mental health treatment, Grippy Sox Club has helped normalize conversations around mental health and reduce the stigma that often prevents people from seeking help.

Program Offerings

  • Direct entry package programs for mental institutions
  • High-quality, appealing merchandise and clothing
  • Nationwide partnerships with mental health facilities
  • Stigma reduction through positive brand association

Social Impact

Grippy Sox Club's innovative approach to mental health merchandise has had a measurable impact on reducing stigma around mental health treatment. By creating products that patients are proud to wear and use, the company helps normalize mental health care and encourages more people to seek the help they need without shame or embarrassment.

Business Model

The direct entry package program provides mental institutions with turnkey merchandise solutions, making it easy for facilities to offer quality products to their patients. This B2B model has enabled rapid scaling across the country while maintaining the brand's core mission of destigmatizing mental health treatment through thoughtful, appealing design.
